AI Builder-in-Residence — Summit Partners, Base Camp Program (Boston, MA)

A full-time residency building AI tools across the firm and its portfolio. From month one you work directly with Summit's investment team to build AI tools that matter — research, monitoring, and business operations, including automating memo drafting, executive search, and sourcing.

What you'll do:
- Identify high-value AI opportunities inside the firm and build tools that improve day-to-day operations.
- Take frameworks you've built internally and deploy them across portfolio companies; build repeatable systems that scale.

Candidate profile:
- Builders first — comfortable with Python, LLM APIs, and agent frameworks. Shipped work and source code matter more than credentials.
- Systems thinkers — design for reuse; think about how a solution scales across teams before writing code.
- Business curious — bridge engineering and business; understand the outcomes that matter to operators and investors.
- High-agency — comfortable with ambiguity; make decisions and move forward without perfect specifications.

Qualifications:
- 0–2 years of professional experience
- Background in computer science, engineering, or a quantitative field
- Demonstrated experience building with LLMs, agents, or AI-powered applications
- Strong written and verbal communication; able to work independently in a fast-moving environment
